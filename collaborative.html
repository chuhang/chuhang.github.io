Localization in Forests

Office of Naval Research project
Collaborate with MIT Lincoln Lab

During my visiting at TTIC, I worked on a collaborative project on localization in forests. We explored the possibilities of two ideas: 1. We want to localize a person, who has a camera as well as an UAV, within an existing image database of the forest environment. 2. We want to localize a person, who has a camera, within an existing LIDAR map of the forest environment. My research provided some preliminary study for the project.
