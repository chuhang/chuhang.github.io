About me

I am a Master of Science student at Electrical and Computer Engineering of Cornell University. I am advised by Prof. Tsuhan Chen, co-advised by Prof. Ashutosh Saxena.

I also take advice from Prof. Noah Snavely, Dr. Andrew Gallagher, Dr.(Prof. now) Amir Sadovnik, and Dr.(Prof. now) Ruogu Fang.

I received my bachelor's degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I worked closely with Prof. Weiyao Lin and Prof. Wun-Jun Li.

I enjoy doing computer vision stuff. I am particularly interested in technologies for understanding and reconstructing the world. I love ideas that are simple but powerful.

I am graduating in next year (2015). Computer vision is simply so much fun, I can see so many ideas and possibilities quietly laying there, unexplored. How can I leave now? So yeah, I decide to pursue a PhD.
