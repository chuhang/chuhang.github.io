About me

I am a Master of Science student at Electrical and Computer Engineering of Cornell University. I am advised by Prof. Tsuhan Chen, co-advised by Prof. Ashutosh Saxena.

I also take advice from Prof. Noah Snavely, Dr. Andrew Gallagher, Prof. Amir Sadovnik, and Prof. Ruogu Fang.

I received my bachelor's degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I worked closely with Prof. Weiyao Lin and Prof. Wun-Jun Li.

I enjoy doing computer vision research. I am particularly interested in understanding and reconstructing the world. I am graduating in 2015. Computer vision is simply so much fun, I can see so many ideas and possibilities quietly laying there, unexplored. I decided to apply for PhD.

I am now visiting Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago, working with Prof. Matthew Walter in Robotics.
