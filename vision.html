Group Activity Recognition

We propose a Heat-Map method to model object trajectories in group events. Our model captures rich temporal-spatial information of the extracted trajectories. Comparing to previous overall-statistical feature based methods, our method can not only recognize more complex types of group events, but also improve recognition accuracy.

Papers: ACM MM 12 TCSVT 13

Dataset: Download

Visual GPS Refinement in Urban Environments

GPS can be inaccurate in "urban canyon". We present an framework for refining GPS location and estimate the camera orientation, using a single building image, a 2D map, and a noisy GPS location.

Paper: CVPR Workshop 14

Photo Aesthetic Evaluation

A joint research project of Cornell AMP Lab and Huawei Technologies. Our goal is to produce an accurate classifier that tells users how beatiful their photoes are, and as well offers editing advices such as cropping and color tuning.
