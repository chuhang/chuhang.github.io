Visual GPS Refinement in Urban Environments

GPS can be inaccurate in "urban canyon". We present an framework for refining GPS location and estimate the camera orientation, using a single building image, a 2D map, and a noisy GPS location.

Group Activity Recognition

We propose a Heat-Map method to model object trajectories in group events. Our model includes rich temporal-spatial information of the extracted trajectories, which is capable of both recognizing complex group events and recognizing events with improved accuracy.
